ILLEGAL ALIENS IN PARIS

And they're hungry for baguettes! Yesterday at nine o'clock European Standard Time, a UFO landed next to the Eiffel Tower in Paris, France. The UFO was oddly shaped like the famous landmark, and it is believed the flying saucer mistook le Champs de Mars for a landing strip. Parisians and tourists alike ran for their lives as the spaceship approached silently in the morning sky.
President Macron has declared a state of emergency, and the area surrounding the Eiffel Tower has been closed to the public. At the moment, we don't want to take any actions that may seem hostile to the aliens. said the French president.
In response, most French postal workers have gone on strike, demanding the aliens' retirement age be set at 61 years old. President Trump declared at a press conference yesterday evening that it was typical for Europeans to allow mass immigration and that the Eiffel Tower design was overrated, really ugly when you think about it.
Meanwhile, all the cats and dogs in the French capital have disappeared, leading many to believe those aliens actually are eating the pets.
CNN reporter Cooper Andersson asked President Trump about his thoughts on the subject: Then they're probably Haitians!, the US president answered.
